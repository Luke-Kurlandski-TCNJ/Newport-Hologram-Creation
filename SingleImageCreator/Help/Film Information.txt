Film Information
@author Luke Kurlandski
November 11, 2019

This information specificaly concerns the Film Information.

Image Width on Film:
	Enter, in meters, how wide the image should appear on the hologram. 
	This information is used to determine the path of motion for the motor.

Image Height on Film:
	Enter, in meters, how tall the image should appear on the hologram. 
	This information is used to determine the path of motion for the motor.

Estimate Spot Size:
	Enter, in microns, the diameter of a typical grating that will be produced under the current conditions.
	This information is purely visual and is not used in any computations.
